Under weight

focus on healthy weight gain by increasing calorie-dense, nutrient-rich foods.
1. Eat More Calories – Include nuts, seeds, dairy, healthy oils, and whole grains.
2. Increase Protein Intake – Consume eggs, chicken, fish, tofu, and legumes to build muscle.
3.Eat Frequently – Have 5-6 small meals a day with snacks like yogurt, cheese, and nut
4. Drink High-Calorie Beverages – Opt for smoothies, milk, or fresh juices instead of plain water.
5.Exercise Regularly – Strength training helps in healthy weight gain rather than just adding fat.

Normal weight

1.Follow a balanced diet with proteins, carbs, and healthy fats.
2. Include fruits, vegetables, lean meats, whole grains, and dairy in meals.
3. Stay hydrated and limit processed foods.
4.Exercise regularly to maintain fitness and metabolism.

Over weight

1. Reduce refined carbs (white rice, sugar) and processed foods.
2.Increase fiber intake (vegetables, fruits, whole grains) to stay full longer.
3. Focus on lean proteins like fish, eggs, and legumes.
4. Cut down on sugary drinks and high-fat foods.
5. Engage in cardio and strength exercises for fat loss.

Obese

1. Eat low-calorie, nutrient-rich foods like salads, lean proteins, and whole grains.
2. Cut down on sugary foods, fried items, and excessive salt.
3. Practice portion control and avoid emotional eating.
4. Follow a structured exercise plan combining cardio and resistance training.
5. Stay consistent with lifestyle changes for long-term results.

Extreem weight

1.Consult a nutritionist for a personalized meal plan.
2.Prioritize whole, unprocessed foods and eliminate junk food.
3.Strictly monitor portion sizes and caloric intake.
4.Engage in low-impact exercises like walking and swimming initially.
5.Gradually progress to more intensive workouts under professional guidance.
